This lab trains defenders to detect spear phishing using behavioral, metadata, and workflow-based indicators.

---

## Objectives
- Identify anomalies in email content  
- Detect deviations from normal communication  
- Understand workflow authenticity  
- Spot high-risk pretext structures  

---

## Steps

### 1. Analyze Sender & Metadata
Check for:
- Incorrect sender domain  
- Forwarding chain manipulation  
- SPF/DKIM inconsistencies  
- External timestamp anomalies  

### 2. Evaluate Writing Style
Observe:
- Tone mismatches  
- Formality inconsistencies  
- Uncommon phrasing  
- Grammar anomalies  

### 3. Compare Against Organizational Workflow
Determine if:
- The request fits timing norms  
- The sender typically issues such requests  
- The message aligns with internal templates  

### 4. Identify High-Risk Indicators
Examples:
- Urgency language  
- Attachment pressure  
- Credential collection attempts  
- Redirect links with mismatch domains  

---

- Annotated sample email  
- List of triggered alerts
