# Lab 1 â€” OSINT Corporate Mapping
This lab teaches how attackers and defenders map an organization's public footprint to understand communication patterns, business structure, and potential phishing angles.

---

## Objectives
- Identify publicly exposed corporate structure
- Analyze communication patterns
- Extract non-sensitive data useful to defenders
- Understand how OSINT informs spear phishing

---

## Steps

### 1. Identify Public-Facing Departments
Use:
- Company website
- LinkedIn pages
- Public press releases
- Vendor documentation

Record:
- Department names
- Management structure
- Communication style

### 2. Map Communication Culture
Note:
- Signature formats  
- Tone and formality  
- Typical greeting/closing styles  
- Frequency of announcements  

### 3. Analyze Business Cycles
Look at:
- Hiring cycles  
- Fiscal quarter events  
- Annual reviews  
- Vendor updates  

### 4. Document Indicators Useful for Blue Team
- Unusual messaging timing  
- Signature inconsistencies  
- Off-cycle financial requests  
- Unexpected sender titles  

---

## Deliverables
- OSINT notes  
- Communication-style summary  
- Potential defensive recommendations
