# Lab 2 — Pretext Development
This lab focuses on constructing realistic and defensively useful pretexts modeled after real organizational workflows.

---

## Objectives
- Build realistic narrative templates
- Analyze workflow-based phishing angles
- Translate red-team insights into defensive education

---

## Steps

### 1. Pick a Business Workflow to Model
Examples:
- IT password reset flow  
- HR policy updates  
- Vendor invoice follow-up  
- Internal approval requests  

### 2. Analyze Workflow Authenticity
Check:
- Tone  
- Format  
- Expected timing  
- Typical sender identity  

### 3. Write a High-Realism Pretext (Educational Use Only)
Include:
- A believable request  
- Context that fits the workflow  
- Internal-style formatting  

### 4. Identify “Defender Red Flags”
List suspicious indicators such as:
- Unexpected timing  
- Incorrect role titles  
- Odd URL structure  
- Language inconsistencies  

---

## Deliverables
- Pretext draft  
- Blue-team red-flag list  
- Workflow authenticity justification
