# Reconnaissance Insights for Blue Teams

This file reframes red-team recon as actionable defensive improvements.

---

## 1. Corporate Footprint Awareness
Blue teams should track:

- exposed public services  
- certificate transparency logs  
- cloud-based assets  
- vendor dependencies  

---

## 2. Business Process Exposure
Recon shows which processes are vulnerable:

- HR cycles  
- Procurement workflows  
- Compliance deadlines  
- Payment systems  

---

## 3. Email Culture Mapping
Understanding email habits helps detect anomalies:

- tone  
- signatures  
- communication timing  
- department-specific vocabulary  

---

## 4. Defensive Application
Use recon insights to:

- strengthen training  
- improve phishing detection  
- refine alerting workflows  

---
