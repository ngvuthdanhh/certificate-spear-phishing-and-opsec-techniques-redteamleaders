# Writing Style OPSEC

Understanding how writing style can unintentionally reveal identity or operator linkage.

---

## 1. Linguistic Fingerprints
Identity leaks through:

- idioms  
- spelling consistency  
- punctuation style  
- sentence rhythm  
- preferred transitions  

---

## 2. Style-Masking Techniques
- Use neutral corporate tone  
- Avoid idiomatic expressions  
- Follow standardized templates  
- Maintain consistent formality  

---

## 3. Why It Matters
Writing style can enable:

- attribution  
- behavioral clustering  
- operator deanonymization  

Therefore, mitigating linguistic fingerprints is essential in any OPSEC-aware environment.
