# OPSEC Failure Casebook

Documenting common OPSEC mistakes observed in training, audits, and public case studies.

---

## 1. Pattern-of-Life Leaks
Behavioral patterns reveal:

- timezone  
- daily schedule  
- working habits  

---

## 2. Writing Style Fingerprints
Operators unintentionally leak:

- idioms  
- punctuation habits  
- sentence structure  
- regional language traits  

---

## 3. Metadata Exposure
Documents often leak:

- creator name  
- application version  
- timezone metadata  

---

## 4. Infrastructure Reuse
Reusing patterns like:

- hosting providers  
- domain naming conventions  
- operational timing  

can compromise anonymity.

---

## 5. Account Cross-Use
Mixing personal and operational accounts can fully deanonymize an identity.
