# 09. Red Team Phishing Lifecycle

The spear phishing process follows a structured cycle:

1. **Planning the objective**  
2. **OSINT and target profiling**  
3. **Pretext and lure development**  
4. **Infrastructure provisioning**  
5. **Delivery preparation**  
6. **Execution**  
7. **Tracking and adjusting**  
8. **Reporting and clean-up**  

Every step must align with the **Rules of Engagement**.
