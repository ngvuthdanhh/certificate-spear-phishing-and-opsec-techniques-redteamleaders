# Engagement Guidelines
This document outlines operational rules and boundaries for any research or simulation related to spear phishing and OPSEC awareness.

---

## Purpose
To define safe, ethical, and controlled engagement guidelines that prevent misuse and ensure any simulation adheres to legal and organizational standards.

---

## Scope
- Internal security training  
- Defensive research  
- Workflow analysis  
- Awareness and educational exercises  

---

## Rules
1. No real-world spear phishing attempts  
2. No unauthorized data collection  
3. All simulations must use fabricated identities  
4. All research must remain defensive and educational  

---

## Responsibilities
- Analysts must follow ethical boundaries  
- Supervisors must approve exercises  
- Documentation must remain internal and anonymized
