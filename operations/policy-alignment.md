# Policy Alignment
Ensuring all simulations and educational content align with internal and international cybersecurity standards.

---

## Relevant Standards
- ISO/IEC 27001  
- NIST SP 800-61 & 800-115  
- Local organizational security policy  
- Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)  

---

## Alignment Checklist
- [ ] Does the scenario violate any real-world communication policies?  
- [ ] Are fabricated identities used?  
- [ ] Is sensitive data avoided entirely?  
- [ ] Are results logged only for educational value?  

---

## Documentation Requirements
- Clear justification  
- Minimal data collection  
- Compliance notes
