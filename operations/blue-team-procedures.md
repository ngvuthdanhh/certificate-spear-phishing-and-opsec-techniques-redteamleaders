# Blue Team Procedures
Defensive actions and analysis processes for handling potential spear-phishing attempts.

---

## Step 1: Initial Triage
- Validate sender identity  
- Check metadata consistency  
- Review message structure  

---

## Step 2: Behavioral Analysis
- Compare with known communication styles  
- Verify timing vs workflow norms  
- Assess urgency predictors  

---

## Step 3: Technical Analysis
- SPF/DKIM evaluations  
- URL and domain inspection  
- Attachment behavior inspection  

---

## Step 4: Response
- Report to SOC or security team  
- Isolate suspicious message  
- Provide awareness feedback  

---

## Deliverables
- Triage log  
- Detection notes  
- Recommended mitigation steps
